Last updated: March 13th 2022

We, 2048: Puzzle of Twos, are committed to respecting your privacy and the integrity and security of your personal data.

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile game 2048: Puzzle of Twos.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of the policy. If we make changes that are material, we will use reasonable efforts to notify you (such as by placing an in-game notice or a push-notification). We encourage you to check this Privacy Policy regularly for updates, so that you know our current practices.

By downloading, accessing and/or using this game, you acknowledge the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

The personal data we collect will depend on the circumstances and the features you are using.

A. DATA YOU PROVIDE TO US

  • Contact information (such as name and email address) - only if you initiate the contact
  • Authentication information - only if you sign in with google account (then we use chosen google email and display name from public profile)
  • Your messages such as player support tickets or reviews
  • Other data you choose to provide us when you contact us (through email, social media or any other channels).

B. DATA WE COLLECT AUTOMATICALLY

  • Data and analytics about your use of this game (e.g. your game progress, game score, the date and time you used a game, session length, features you have used like number of used undos, your interaction with advertisements)
  • Some or all of the following information about your device: unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFV for iOS devices and Android ID for Android devices); IP address; country of install (mapped from IP address); device manufacturer and model platform type (iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, etc.) and the operating system and version running on your system or device; language; CPU information such as model, the number of CPUs present, frequency, and instruction set support flags; the graphics card type and vendor name; graphics card driver name and version (e.g., “nv4disp.dll 6.10.93.71”); which graphics API is in use (e.g., “OpenGL 2.1” or “Direct3D 9.0c”); amount of system and video RAM present; current screen resolution; version of the Unity Editor used to create the game; sensor flags (e.g., device support for gyroscope, touch pressure or accelerometer); application or bundle identification (“app ID”) of the game installed; unique advertising identifiers provided for iOS and Android devices (e.g., IDFA or Android Ad ID); and a checksum of all the data that gets sent to verify that it transmitted correctly.

2. WHY WE COLLECT AND USE YOUR DATA

We process data necessary to:

  • Facilitate, operate, provide and maintain this game
  • Recognize you when you return to this game
  • Recognize your scores in the Top scores lists.
  • Respond to your comments and questions and provide player support
  • Serve you generic non-personalized advertisements in the game using third party advertising networks

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR DATA

We share your data with following third parties:

  • Google Firebase/Analytics - for general analytics, detection and causes of crashes and for storing your game scores
  • Google AdMob and AppLovin - which may share some of mentioned data with ad publishers

Links to the privacy policies of third-party service providers used by us:

  • AdMob
  • AppLovin
  • Google Firebase/Analytics

4. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

This game is global by nature and your data can therefore be transferred and processed in countries other than the country in which you are resident.

DATA RETENTION

We keep only data from section 1 A (information you have provided us through email/support tickets/social media). If you want that data deleted, please contact us and specify which data you want to be deleted.

If you choose to sign in via Google there is an option to delete account which would delete all your personal data immediately from our system.

Third parties listed in section 3 will keep data shared with them according to their policies which you can look up on their web sites.

Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for use only by those aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
